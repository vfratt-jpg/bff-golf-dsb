Our Golf Journey

The epic tale of friendship, competition, and legendary championships that began in 1990

1990s

The Paultz Legacy Begins

The journey started several years after the famed Paultz keg parties that evolved into party/golf outings. The first tournament was the Paultz, named after a former ABA basketball player that Dave Petty loved. It featured a four-man scramble format with teams drawn from B, B, C, and D level players.

1990s

Bryce Virginia Adventures

Ted's sister had a timeshare at the Bryce Ski and Golf resort in Bryce, Virginia. The timing was always around Good Friday in early April - not ideal since it wasn't always cold enough for snow to ski and not always warm enough for golf. When it rained/slushed ice, forget about Jimmy playing in that! Ted, aka "The Godfather," invited several friends to the resort to play golf.

2010

The Brycehurst Invitational is Born

The Brycehurst Invitational was born when Jimmy, who had a Pinehurst CC membership, invited the group to have their Bryce outing there instead. The name is a combination of their long-running Bryce golf outing and their new venue, Pinehurst! The inaugural winner was Ken Sympson. Dave Petty and John did not make this first year in Pinehurst, and Dave sadly passed away that same year.

First Brycehurst Champion: Ken Sympson
2011

The Petty Cup Memorial

Following Dave Petty's passing, the Brycehurst Invitational adopted "The Petty Cup" trophy in his honor. This became the flagship championship, maintaining the tournament's prestige while memorializing their beloved friend and founding member.

First Petty Cup Champion: Kevin Britt
2020

The Championship Wars Begin

The Championship of the World (COW) trophy was created by Paul from a trophy he found at a garage sale, in protest of Jim's early Petty Cup dominance. It came with its own set of rules designed to make it difficult for Jim to win. In response, Jim created the Undisputed Championship of the World (UCOW) - the same trophy with a piece of tape and "UCOW" written on it!

COW & UCOW Era Begins
2022

The Trophy Collection Expands

John wanted to join the fun and created a low net competition called the Twice as Much Cup (TAMC), which is actually a brown jug. You can only qualify by staying in JB's camper - Vince was the first participant when he and John camped at Bryce. Dom soon joined the club. Ken added a low gross game he named "Suck it Up Cup" (SUC), though to this day there's no actual cup or trophy representing the competition.

TAMC & SUC Championships Launch
