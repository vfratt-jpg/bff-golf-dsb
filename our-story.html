Ted's sister owned a timeshare at Bryce Ski and Golf Resort in Virginia, always available around Good Friday in early April. 2010 - The Revolution

üèÜ Brycehurst Invitational Born

The game changed forever when Jimmy, with his Pinehurst Country Club membership, invited the group to hold their annual outing at the legendary Pinehurst Resort instead of Bryce. The name "Brycehurst" perfectly combined their cherished Bryce tradition with their prestigious new venue. Ken Sympson claimed the inaugural championship, marking the beginning of the modern era.

2011 - The Memorial

üíô The Petty Cup Legacy

Following the passing of beloved founder Dave Petty, the Brycehurst Invitational adopted "The Petty Cup" trophy in his honor. This became our flagship championship, preserving Dave's memory while elevating the tournament's prestige. Kevin Britt earned the honor of being the first Petty Cup champion, beginning a tradition that continues to this day.

Competitive fire reached new heights when Paul created the Championship of the World (COW) from a garage sale trophy, specifically designed to challenge Jim's Petty Cup dominance.
